## Packages
framer-motion | Complex animations, scroll-triggered reveals, and page transitions
clsx | Utility for constructing className strings conditionally
tailwind-merge | Utility for merging Tailwind CSS classes safely

## Notes
The design is dark-mode focused with a cinematic aesthetic.
Icons are provided by `lucide-react` (already in base stack).
Routing is handled by `wouter`.
Data fetching is handled by `@tanstack/react-query`.
